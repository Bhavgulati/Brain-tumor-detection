About This Platform

NeuroScan AI detects brain tumors from MRI scans across four categories: No Tumor, Meningioma (slow-growing, usually benign), Glioma (requires urgent attention), and Pituitary (usually manageable). It also detects Skin Cancer from dermoscopy images.

Common symptoms: headaches, seizures, vision changes, personality changes, weakness.
